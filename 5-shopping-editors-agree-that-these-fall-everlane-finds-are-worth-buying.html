5 Shopping Editors Agree That These Fall Everlane Finds Are Worth Buying

September 21, 2024
blog

As a shopping writer, I’m constantly keeping abreast of the latest fashion must-haves. My fellow shopping editors and I have tested out tons of sweaters, blazers, jeans, and boots — likely more than the average person — so we know when we’ve struck gold on a piece that’s going to be a staple in our outfit rotations. 

Over time, we’ve come across products that grab our attention so much that we can’t stop wearing them, and we want to scream their praises from the rooftops or, in this case, to you. One retailer that’s home to five shopping editors’  favorite timeless wardrobe finds is Everlane. 

Whether you’re on the hunt for an evergreen blazer, a wear-with-anything boot, or a trench coat that will outlive all the trends, you can find all of these and more on the brand's virtual racks. 

Keep scrolling to see what our editors are loving this fall. 

Editor-Loved Everlane Picks

The Chelsea Boot

Lauren Fischer, Partnerships Editor: I've been eyeing a new pair of Chelsea boots to add to my cold-weather footwear collection and was looking for an option that offered support and comfort with a low heel to elevate my short 5-foot stature. The Everlane Chelsea Boot slips on and off easily, and they didn't require a long break-in period. In fact, I wore them to day-long outings the first weekend I got them, and they already felt molded to my foot, as if I'd been wearing them for months. I like that the rubber sole is durable and good for all types of weather now that winter is approaching, while the leather upper material is extremely soft and smooth. My feet never felt tired or achy, even when standing and walking in them, and they coordinate well with straight-leg jeans and my fall midi dresses.

The Italian Wool Oversized Blazer

Ariel Scotti, Partnerships Editorial Director: I'm obsessed with this oversized blazer. I'm always a little anxious about ordering one without trying it on first, but the fit on this is impeccable. The shoulders aren't too broad, the waist isn't too boxy, and the sleeves aren't too long, but there's enough room to layer this over a sweater on colder days. The wool is surprisingly soft and super cozy without feeling scratchy at all. I also love the slight herringbone pattern that isn't too in-your-face but subtle enough for added interest. I'm going to wear this jacket forever.

The ReNew Long Liner

Bridget DeWald, Partnerships Editor: As someone who sits at a computer all day, I make sure to get my steps in before or after work — no matter the temperature outside. This coat has been my go-to outerwear piece this season because it's the ideal thickness for fall temperatures and hits below the knee for extra coverage and warmth. In addition to wearing it on my ‘hot girl walks,’ I've been throwing the jacket over nearly every outfit, including jeans and a hoodie or a maxi dress with knee-high boots. Pro-tip: Before selecting my size, I read the reviews and decided to size down based on what other shoppers were saying. I'm happy with the size I chose because I can still layer bulky sweaters and hoodies underneath without swallowing my 5-foot frame.

The Tencel Oversized Blazer

Ali Faccenda, Partnerships Writer: After moving into a studio apartment, my closet space dictated my wardrobe. Since then, I’ve worked hard at building out a capsule wardrobe of classic pieces that I can mix and match and that will still be wearable and stylish through the years. Blazers are one of my favorite layering pieces, and this one from Everlane is at the top of my most-worn list. I love a boxier fit since I can layer them with thicker long sleeves and sweaters without feeling constricted. The ash brown complements all of the other neutral-toned basics in my closet, and I’ve loved dressing it up over skirts and knee-high boots or down over a casual hoodie and leggings. The quality is amazing, and it’s a great outerwear option for warmer fall days. 

The Cotton Long Trench Coat

Meg Schaltegger, Partnerships Writer: I love how this Everlane trench so easily elevates any outfit. It’s versatile enough to toss on with a dress and boots but equally stylish with jeans and street-style sneakers (my personal favorite combo). I wore the trench coat nonstop while in London and received so many compliments on the style.
