    Kim Kardashian encountered one of her scariest experiences ever as she went on one of Disneyland's spookier rides, the Tower of Terror, with her sister, Kourtney.

    The Tower of Terror is a ride at MGM Studios which drops riders in the dark as they ride in an elevator, and from Kim's reaction it looks like the reality show star wasn't expecting the surprise drop! Kourtney posted a hilarious video of the moment on Instagram, captioning the clip: "I took her on Tower of Terror for the first time."

    Kourtney laughed at her sister's reaction

    The star screams as the ride drops, shouting: "Oh my god I hate this! I hate this!"

    The family visited Disneyland to celebrate the third birthday of Kim's daughter North West. Although the toddler was too young to join her mum and aunt on the scary ride, there were plenty of more age-appropriate attractions for her to enjoy. Kim has been keeping her Instagram followers updated about what they've been doing on the trip, with one snap showing the birthday girl in a bright pink princess dress while sat by Bambi and Thumper. She wrote: "Thank you @Disneyland for making all of my princess dreams come true today."

    The family celebrated North's birthday at Disneyland

    The star also shared a video of herself chatting to little Nori, telling her she wasn't allowed to get any bigger than she already was. She wrote: "She promised me she wouldn't get any bigger. I can't believe my baby girl is 3 years old today!!!! Northie I love you so much I can't even explain it! Happy Birthday to my favorite mermaid!!!"

    In another video, she is showering her daughter in kisses while on a boat with her husband Kanye. The caption reads: "I will never stop kissing you and loving you and annoying you."

    Kourtney posted a photo of the firework display over the Magic Kingdom, simply captioning the snap with a crystal ball emoji.
