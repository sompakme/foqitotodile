Ralph Lauren no longer offers a student discount online. However, students can still save on their next purchase by browsing our Ralph Lauren promo codes.  

When does Ralph Lauren have sales?

Start shopping the Ralph Lauren sales during seasonal events for the best savings. Currently, you can shop up to 40% off when you spend $125 on a purchase, as part of their holiday savings. It’s also worth checking their dedicated ‘sales’ page on the Ralph Lauren website all year round.

Does Ralph Lauren run a Cyber Monday sale?

Yes. Head to the Ralph Lauren website to save up to 40% this Black Friday and Cyber Monday. It's a great way to make some extra savings.
