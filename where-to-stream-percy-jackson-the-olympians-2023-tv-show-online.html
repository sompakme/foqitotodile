More than 10 years since Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Sea of Monsters underperformed in theaters in 2013, Disney has rebooted the franchise with a new TV series — and you can now stream the entire first season online.

All eight episodes of Percy Jackson and the Olympians are available to stream on Disney+, and the first episode is available on Hulu.

A Disney+ subscription is required to watch Percy Jackson and the Olympians. The streaming service is $7.99 per month ($79.99 per year) with ads or $13.99 per month (or $139.99 per year) without ads. It’ also home to movies and TV shows under Disney’s umbrella, including Marvel Studios, Lucasfilm (Star Wars), Pixar Animation, National Geographic and more.

The new reboot series follows Percy Jackson (Walker Scobell), a 12-year-old boy who discovers he’s a demigod. After he learns to use his new divine powers, Zeus tasks him with finding his master lightning bolt, which has been stolen. With the help of his friends, Annabeth (Leah Sava Jeffries) and Grover (Aryan Simhadri), Jackson must now get the weapon, return it and bring order back to Olympus.

Where to Buy the Percy Jackson and the Olympians Novel Online

Percy Jackson and the Olympians is based on the best-selling novel The Lightning Thief by Rick Riordan. It’s the first entry in the Percy Jackson book series, which was released in June 2005.

It’s available at Amazon in hardcover for $16 (reg. $19), in paperback for $7 (reg. $9), as a Kindle ebook for $6 and as a free audiobook for Audible members.

How to Watch The Percy Jackson Movies Online

With the hopes of starting a new Harry Potter-esque film franchise, Disney released the film adaption of Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Lightning Thief in 2010, which starred Logan Lerman, Alexandra Daddario and Brandon T. Jackson.

While it was a box office hit that received a sequel, Percy Jackson and the Olympians: Sea of Monsters, the Mouse House wasn’t confident that a third movie would be as successful. All plans for Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Titan’s Curse were canceled in 2014.

However, both The Lightning Thief and Sea of Monsters movies are available to stream on Disney+.

Watch Percy Jackson and the Olympians on Disney+: Best Streaming Deals

One of the best deals to stream Percy Jackson and the Olympians is with the Disney Duo bundle, which comes with the ad-supported versions of Hulu and Disney+ for $9.99 per month — that’s a nearly 40 percent savings altogether.

Additionally, the Disney Trio offers Hulu, Disney+ and ESPN+, all with ads, for $14.99 per month — about 45 percent off compared to signing up for separate subscriptions. You can even go somewhat commercial-free with Disney Trio Premium. It includes the ad-free versions of Hulu and Disney+ and the ad-supported version of ESPN+ for $24.99 per month.

If you’re a cord-cutter, Hulu + Live TV comes with Disney+ and ESPN+ for $76.99 per month for the ad-supported package, or $81.99 per month to get the ad-free versions of Hulu and Disney+.
