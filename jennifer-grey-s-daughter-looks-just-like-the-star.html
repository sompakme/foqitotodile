Jennifer Grey's Daughter Looks Just Like The Star

July 20, 2024
blog

There's no doubt that Jennifer Grey and Stella Gregg look related. In December 2021, Grey posted a stunning photo of her daughter on Instagram. She wrote, "i love this person. happy birthday my darling stella #thisis20 @stellagregg slow yer roll, it's going too fast!" With their brown eyes and brown hair, the mother and daughter pair share some striking similarities. Fans couldn't agree more and took to the comments section to talk about the uncanny resemblance. 

One user remarked, "She's your lil mini-beautiful!" Another person wrote, "Happy birthday to this astonishing young lady...who looks so much like her graceful mom...and carries the Greek name Stella ! Beautiful." Fans can see more of Gregg on her Instagram page, which is packed with selfies and candid photos.

While Gregg looks a lot like Grey, she isn't exactly the spitting image of her father, Clark Gregg. However, the two do share one major thing in common — a sense of humor. The two have created numerous funny videos together, which Clark has shared on his TikTok account. In one video, they show off a couple moves before switching outfits. In another video, they lip-sync to audio of the Kardashians and it's downright silly. Like father, like daughter!
