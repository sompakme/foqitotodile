Kid Cudi backs Cassie's claims that in a jealous rage P. Diddy "blew up a man's car" who happened to be Kid Cudi's.

Detailed in the lawsuit, Cassie explains that in a jealous act, Diddy “blew up a man’s car after he learned that he was romantically interested in Ms. Ventura.” The report claims the car belonged to rapper Kid Cudi. “Mr. Combs grew so angry about her dating the rapper Kid Cudi that he said he would blow up the rapper’s car.’ Around that time, the suit says, ‘Kid Cudi’s car exploded in his driveway.’”
