US-based Nollywood actor, Alesh Ola Sanni, recently shared a personal experience with his fans on social media.

The actor in his post recounted a time when a lady spotted him at his place of work; a grocery store, and decided to make a mockery of him.

Sanni said the lady pulled out her phone, called a friend on facetime and made fun of him while ridiculing his job.

"A lady saw me doing Instacart (grocery shopping for customers), and she took her phone to Facetime her friend, saying see your celebrity crush doing this kind of work and the werey will be flaunting on social media, his post read in part."
