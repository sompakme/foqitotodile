You and Kingman: A Not-So-Long Distance Love Affair (with Las Vegas)

Las Vegas, baby! City of lights, gambling delights, and... an overwhelming urge to escape the flashing neon and clinking coins for a breath of fresh air? Hold on, high rollers, before you hop on a plane to Fiji (though that does sound tempting), there's a hidden gem just a stone's throw (well, maybe a good car toss) away: Kingman, Arizona.

Kingman? Who's she? (And how far is she?)

But wait, how far exactly is this Kingman character from the glitzy heart of Las Vegas? Drumroll please... not far at all! We're talking a mere 107-ish miles depending on which road you take. That's like, two long pours of your favorite beverage, a good singalong to cheesy 80s hits, and bam, you're in a whole new state (Arizona, hello!).

So, Vegas is Out? Not Exactly...

Now, don't get us wrong, Vegas is a party waiting to happen. But if you're feeling the desert vibes and want a taste of something different, Kingman awaits. This historic Route 66 town is bursting with charm, from quirky roadside attractions (hello, giant Route 66 shield!) to museums packed with Wild West lore.

Think of it this way: Vegas is the all-night buffet, while Kingman is the delicious, unexpected diner serving up a mean slice of Americana. You can totally do both, my friend! Hit the slots in Vegas, then cruise on over to Kingman for a chilled-out day trip.

Pro Tip: Time your trip right, and you might even catch the Kingman Muffler Men strut their stuff. Yes, you read that right. Muffler Men.

Kingman vs. Vegas: The Quick and Dirty

  • Bright lights, big city: Vegas
  • Quirky charm and historic vibes: Kingman
  • Buffet that never ends: Vegas
  • Route 66 nostalgia: Kingman
  • Showbiz glitz: Vegas
  • Giant Muffler Men: Kingman (There's really no competition here)

Kingman Calling: How Far Will You Go?

So, the next time you're in Vegas and that roulette wheel starts to feel a little too familiar, remember, Kingman is a quick drive away. It's the perfect little escape for a day trip adventure, a chance to explore a different side of the desert, and, of course, to snap a selfie with a giant Muffler Man. (We won't judge).

Ready to hit the road? Here are some quick FAQs to get you started:

How to get to Kingman from Las Vegas?

By car: It's an easy drive via I-11 S/ US-93 S. Just follow the signs for Kingman and enjoy the desert scenery!

How long does it take to drive from Las Vegas to Kingman?

About 1 hour and 45 minutes, depending on traffic and how many times you stop to sing along to classic road trip anthems.

What are some things to do in Kingman?

Explore the historic Route 66 district, visit the Mohave Museum of History and Arts, or marvel at the quirky roadside attractions (including those oh-so-grammable Muffler Men).

How hot does it get in Kingman?

Kingman gets hot in the summer, so plan your trip accordingly and pack plenty of water.

Is Kingman worth visiting?

Absolutely! Kingman offers a unique blend of history, quirky charm, and desert beauty. It's the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of Las Vegas.
