TORI Kelly's husband has shared some encouraging news about his wife.

The singer was rushed to the hospital after suffering blood clots.

On Wednesday, Tori's husband, Andre Murillo, shared an update on his personal Instagram account.

"Tori is smiling again and feeling stronger," he wrote. "Not fully out of the woods, but we see the sun. Just waiting for a few more answers."

He then shared a message of appreciation with all of Tori's fans.

"Your love and kindness has been overwhelming. Thank you so much!"

PASSED OUT

Tori, 30, was in downtown Los Angeles on Sunday with friends when she suddenly reported that her heart rate had picked up, according to TMZ.

Sources told the outlet that she passed out a short time later and was "out for a while."

Her friends ensured that she was transported from the restaurant to Cedars-Sinai hospital where she was treated.

She was treated for the clots, which doctors discovered around several of her vital organs.

EARLY SUCCESS

Victoria Lauren Kelly was born on December 14, 1992 in Wildomar, California, and is an American singer-songwriter and actress.

She found fame in 2010 when she made it through to Hollywood week in the ninth series of American Idol.

While she didn’t make it any further in the talent show, she continued to pursue music, releasing her self-produced EP called Handmade Songs by Tori Kelly in 2012.

The following year she signed with Capitol Records who released her first major-label record, an EP called Foreword in 2013.

Her first album Unbreakable Smile, issued in 2015, made it to No. 2 in the Billboard chart.

In 2016, Kelly landed a gig voicing Meena in the films Sing and Sing 2.

Her second studio album Hiding Place, reached number 35 in the Billboard chart in 2018, which won her two Grammy Awards - Best Gospel Album and Best Gospel Performance/Song.

In 2019 she released her third studio album called Inspired by True Events which was followed by her fourth the following year called A Tori Kelly Christmas.
