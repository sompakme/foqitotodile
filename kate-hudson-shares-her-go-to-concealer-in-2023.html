Kate Hudson Shares Her Go-To Concealer in 2023

August 21, 2024
blog

Actress Kate Hudson is never one to shy away from sharing her beauty go-tos, and she's made it even easier for fans to snag her favorites by posting a video of her five-minute morning routine on Instagram. While a number of products contribute to Hudson's signature glow, one of the stand-outs in her rotation is Monika Blunder’s Blunder Cover, which the star loves using to conceal dark circles under her eyes.

Monika Blunder Blunder Cover All-in-One Foundation

Credit: Nordstrom

The versatile, two-in-one product has a buildable, creamy formula that makes application a breeze, especially since it can be used as either a foundation or brightening under-eye concealer. Plus, as the Blunder Cover is available in a variety of shade options, you can also opt to choose one that's slightly darker than your natural skin tone to create a seamless contour—which adds yet another benefit to this top-rated product.

One satisfied Nordstrom customer writes, “I've been following Monika Blunder since before she came out with this. I bought it the minute it was released and have used it DAILY since. Literally. It's the perfect consistency for whatever you need...either concealing or to be used like foundation. It does not break me out either (I have SUPER sensitive skin). Please don't ever change the formula as there is NOTHING like this on the market."
