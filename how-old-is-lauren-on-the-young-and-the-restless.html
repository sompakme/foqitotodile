How old is Lauren on The Young and the Restless?

July 22, 2024
blog

LAUREN Fenmore has been a staple on The Young and the Restless for a long time now.

In fact, the actress who plays her, Tracey Bregman, joined the show back in 1983.

Did Lauren on The Young and the Restless leave the show?

Tracey, who originally read for the role of Patty Williams according to the show’s fandom wiki, was on contract for the soap opera from 1983 to 1995, playing Lauren Fenmore Baldwin.

She also appeared for a very brief run from June 15 to June 26, 2000.

Tracey returned to the show on August 24, 2001.

In addition to Lauren, Tracey has also portrayed Sheila Carter's sister, Sarah.

How old is Lauren on The Young and the Restless?

Tracey was born in Germany on May 29, 1963.

That makes her 58 years old. 

It seems like Lauren is meant to be the same age as Lauren. However, the Young and the Restless wiki says Lauren is 55; it states she was born in May 1964, which should make her 57. 

Meanwhile, The Bold and the Beautiful wiki says she’s 51.

We’re going to assume those ages are not quite right and that Lauren should also be about 58 years old. 

What other soap operas has Tracey been on?

Most notably, Tracey has played the same character of Lauren on The Bold and the Beautiful as well. 

Plus, she appeared on Days of Our Lives between 1978 and 1980.

In addition to soap opera work, Tracey has portrayed characters in films like Happy Birthday to Me and The Concrete Jungle.

Is Tracey Bregman married?

Tracey married Ron Recht in 1987.

The couple shares two sons, Austin and Landon.

Sadly, they got divorced in 2010.

On CTV’s The Marilyn Denis Show in 2011, Bregman said of her divorce, according to Soap Opera Digest, “I feel so good. I think it’s about me being happy now and kind of putting myself first.”

“I’m 47 and I need to be happy.”
