Ousmane Dembele net worth 2022: What is Dembele's salary?

August 20, 2024
blog

Being one of Barcelona's highest paid players this season, Ousmane Dembele has failed to live up to expectations at the Blaugrana and he is expected to depart the club in the summer, when his contract comes to an end.

The Frenchman decided against renewing his deal and didn't seem willing to part ways with the club in January despite Barcelona being desperate to cash in on him in order to make space in their salary cap and move for another striker in the attempt to rediscover their form under Xavi Hernandez.

In his five years at the Catalans, Dembele won two LaLiga Santander titles, two Copa del Rey trophies and a Supercopa de Espana, but he never stood out despite showing glimpses of his talent at times.

What is Dembele's net worth?

As of January 2022, Dembele's net worth is estimated at around 7.2m euros. The France international moved to Barcelona from Borussia Dortmund back in 2017 in exchange for a reported 105m euros plus another 35m euros in add-ons.

What is Dembele's salary?

In the last year of his contract, Dembele is earning around 250,000 euros per week, which is around 13m euros for the season. This is one of the main reasons why Barcelona struggled to offload Dembele in January, as there were not many potential suitors who could cover his salary and meet his wages demands.

Now, Dembele is free to negotiate a contract with any other team and try his luck elsewhere after turning down Barcelona's contract renewal offers.
