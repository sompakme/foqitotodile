Rosie Huntington-Whiteley's Revolve x Michael Costello Fake Leather Gown

August 19, 2024
blog
  • Rosie Huntington-Whiteley attended the 3rd Annual #REVOLVEawards at Goya Studios in Hollywood, California this week.
  • The model wore a strapless dress from the REVOLVE x Michael Costello collection, which retails for $238 and is still available to purchase.

Rosie Huntington-Whiteley regularly wows us with her red carpet looks, and the model's latest outfit just so happens to be completely affordable, too.

Hitting the red carpet for the #REVOLVEawards in California on Friday night, the model looked seriously stunning in a Michael Costello creation, which is still available to shop for $238. The fake leather look features a boned bodice to allow a cut-away neckline, and also has a dramatic front slit.

The same style dress is also available in emerald sequins and floral velvet, though we're partial to Huntington-Whiteley's edgier choice.

Donato Sardella//Getty Images

Huntington-Whiteley shared some chic behind-the-scenes snaps from the #REVOLVEawards on Instagram, proving that her dress is even versatile as office wear. Well... sort of. Clearly, the model can pull off just about any look.

She also snapped a selfie with fellow model, and Hitch star, Amber Valletta at the event.

Earlier this month, the model stepped out in New York wearing a beautiful little black dress with a major V-neck, which was not safe for work. The off-the-shoulder look was super revealing, and showed a lot of cleavage, but the Marks and Spencer collaborator oozed confidence in the outfit. Huntington-Whiteley paired it with a black clutch and chunky heels.
