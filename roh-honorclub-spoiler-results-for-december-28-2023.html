ROH HonorClub Spoiler Results for December 28, 2023

September 11, 2024
blog

Ring of Honor recently recorded a set of television episodes at the Collision event held in San Antonio, TX. The event featured several notable matches, and here are the results that will air on December 28, 2023. Here are the results sent in from our correspondent at the event:

Griff Garrison secured a victory by pinning Serpentico.

Lance Archer achieved a pinfall victory over JP Harlow.

Tony Nese emerged victorious against an unannounced competitor referred to as the “Texas Kid” by Mark Sterling on the microphone before the match.

Dalton Castle emerged triumphant in his bout against Peter Avalon.

Bryan Keith and The Von Erichs triumphed over The Iron Savages, with the absence of Kevin Von Erich.

Queen Amitala claimed victory over Maya World.

Ethan Page secured a pinfall victory over Johnny TV, thanks to a distraction caused by Dalton Castle, who appeared on stage during the match.

Billie Starkz emerged victorious in her match against Vertvixen.

Taya Valkyrie and The Renegade Twins joined forces to defeat Trish Adora, Lady Frost, and Kiera Hogan.

Mercedes Martinez and Diamante emerged as the victors in their match against Leyla Hirsh and Rachael Ellering.

Red Velvet secured a victory over Alex Gracia.

Lee Moriarty and Shane Taylor secured a win against The Boys and The Infantry.

ROH TV Champion Kyle Fletcher successfully defended his title by defeating Willie Mack.

Hijo Del Vikingo, Komander, and Lee Johnson secured a victory over Gringo Loco and The Workhorsemen.
