Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson shows off his new tattoo

July 17, 2024
blog

Dwayne Johnson is even scarier than before. Photo / Instagram.

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson has the biggest biceps in Hollywood, so his tattoo artist was always going to be in it for the long haul when asked to cover his right arm in ink.

Now, after 30 hours acting as the canvas, the World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) icon turned actor has revealed a stunning new tattoo that transformed a modest piece of work into something you hope you don't run into after dark.

Where Johnson once had a small bull's head now sits a much larger, much more imposing illustration of the animal's skull.

It took 30 hours and four sessions in the chair to finish the masterpiece, which Johnson showed off on Instagram.

"After approximately 30 hours of tattooing (4 sessions) with my good bud and iconic artist @nikkohurtado, the story of my skull is complete," Johnson wrote underneath a video of him revealing the new ink.

"Flow of positive energy is constant thru (sic) the breathe (sic) of life.

"Horns always positioned forward. Not up, but forward — representing forward progress and always at the ready to dig in and dig deep.

"Eye is the mana — the spirit and the power. It's everything. It's where I draw my strength from all things I love and protect.

"As with my Polynesian tattoo on the left side of body, the symbolic mana lays over my heart.

"We all have mana. Our strength. You just have to find it."

Earlier in the year Johnson posted his tattoo's progress — the before-and-after shot showing the dramatic transformation following his penultimate session.
