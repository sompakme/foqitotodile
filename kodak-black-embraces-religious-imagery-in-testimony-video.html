Kodak Black Embraces Religious Imagery In &quot;Testimony&quot; Video

August 07, 2024
blog

As the release date nears for Kodak Black's new album Dying To Live, the Florida rapper continues to share new content from the project. The work is already set to be one of Kodak's most popular as it will likely include his most successful song to date, "ZEZE." His most recent single is "Testimony," which sees the Project Baby getting pretty deep in his lyrics. 

After being released from prison this year, Kodak is reborn and the "Testimony" video is proof of that fact. Instead of mobbing in the streets, the new clip shows the rapper in a religious setting, walking throughout the church as he contemplates the challenges that God has put him through. Different shots allow the viewer to travel through the religious institution with the glass windows, crosses, and gold jewelry being spotlighted. In the end, Kodak even gets baptised as a priest slowly assists the rapper into the water, giving him a second chance to create his legacy.
