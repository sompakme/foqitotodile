Bringing international artists or celebrities to perform in Nigeria is not a small feat as it usually involves millions of naira.

In a recent post on social media, media personality and radio host, Osi, decided to find out how much money would go into getting Grammy-nominated American rapper, Post Malone to come to Nigeria.

N400m and more

Osi disclosed that he reached out to Post Malone's agent and he was thrown back with the demands that must be met.
