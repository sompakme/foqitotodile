While there's no doubt that R&B songstress/model/actress Cassie is nursing a broken heart from her recent split with longtime love Diddy, there's no denying that once she gets herself together she'll be one of the most eligible single ladies in Hollywood. Bursting onto the scene in 2006 with her minimalistic club banger "Me & U" which climbed to No. 3 on the Billboard Hot 100, Cassie has weaved in and out of relevancy with a string of fizzled-out studio features and acting gigs. Shouldering a fledgling solo career, Cassie managed to snag the on-again, off-again interest of Diddy for an impressive 10 years and assuming her role as the queen of Bad Boy Entertainment. 

Speaking to Oxygen just one year before calling it quits for good with the "Last Night" rapper, Cassie made it clear that whatever the future held for her tumultuous longterm relationship, she still believes in love. 

"My lifestyle is so different from everyone else's that I don't know if marriage would be conducive to me sitting here with you right now and having my own stuff," revealed the "Love a Loser" singer. "But I do believe in marriage. I do believe in love."
