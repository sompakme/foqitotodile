Seun Kentebe Shares Memorable Fashion Moment, How He Handles Mistakes: &quot;My Outfit Caused a Stir&quot;

blog
  • Seun Kentebe loves to keep it simple, but it does not prevent him from making fashion statements when necessary
  • The Nollywood star spoke to Legit.ng about the time he made an outstanding fashion impression that got many staring at him
  • He further revealed what he does to ensure that he is not counted among those making fashion mistakes

Nollywood actor Seun Kentebe has mastered his craft and sense of fashion so well that he hardly suffers any wardrobe malfunction.

The movie star noted that he does not step out for functions most times and when he does, his stylist ensures he gives him what he likes.

In this interesting conversation with Legit.ng, Seun shared his memorable fashion moments. He recounted the time he caused a stir with a traditional outfit he wore at an event.

The actor said he is not always available for public events, and this helps him avoid dressing up for assessments.

"I have not experienced any fashion mishaps or embarrassing fashion moments. Maybe it is because I do not step out a lot (laughs)."

What is Seun's memorable fashion moment?

Seun has his experience with being the centre of attention because of a good outfit. He recalled attending an event two years ago. He noted that when he stepped into the venue, some people kept glancing at his traditional outfit.

"My big fashion moment was two years ago (2022) at the premiere of the movie Eleshin Oba at Ebony Life Studios. My stylist got me this traditional aso-oke and it caused quite a stir. I was surprised at the attention it got and it went pretty well."

Seun Kentebe notes career highlights

Legit.ng earlier reported that Seun loves his job as a role interpreter and does not hesitate to do share some of his fond moments.

In a chat with Legit.ng, he noted that featuring in the award-winning series Itura spurred his career to a bigger level.
